What we do

The Community Council is the first tier of local government and is funded by a precept (payment) from the council tax.

It operates at ‘grass roots’ level and enables local people to make improvements to their communities.

Much of its work involves liaison with Monmouthshire County Council which has responsibility for many local amenities.

All planning applications are referred initially to the Community Council for comment before the decision by the County Council.

It also maintains links with other public bodies and community councils.

Community Councillors are elected to represent local residents within the community; to deliver services to meet local needs; and to strive to improve the quality of life in the community they serve.
