Llangybi Allotment Association

Anne Morton

Email: anne.goldsworthy@btinternet.com

Tel:01633 450316

Llangybi Allotment Association has been running since 2012. We are a friendly bunch who enjoy growing things and helping each other out.

We have a water supply on site and the soil has produced some great crops even for a bunch of comparative novices.

Please get in touch if you are interested in taking on a plot or putting your name down on the waiting list if we are full up.

Cost in 2016 - £35 per year.

Andrew and Anne Morton

Email: anne.goldsworthy@btinternet.com

Tel:01633 450316

Everyone is welcome to join our friendly choir. You don’t need to read music.

All you need is a desire for a some good fun and an hour and half a week most Fridays at 7.30 in the Village Hall.

Our maestro/choirmaster/choral arranger/laughter manager is Andrew Morton (www.drewmortonmusic.co.uk)

We sing at Christmas including carol-singing in harmony around the village to raise money for charity.

We also run an informal concert every spring in the Village Hall where we sing everything from arrangements

of spirituals and other songs ( e.g. Steal Away, The Rose) to fun versions of very well-known favourites (e.g.Ugly Duckling).
