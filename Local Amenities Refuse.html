Get your own personalised refuse information

What items can go into the red and purple recycling bags?

Please remember bags need to be placed out by 7am on the morning of collection.

See below for a calendar of refuse collection dates

In the red bag you can recycle:

  • Cardboard
  • Junk mail including envelopes
  • Newspapers and magazines
  • Office paper and shredded paper
  • Drink cartons, eg orange juice etc
  • Telephone directories and catalogues

In the purple bag you can recycle:

  • Aluminium foil/kitchen foil (no crisp packets)
  • Aerosols (must be empty)
  • Plastic bottles
  • Yoghurt pots (please rinse)
  • Margarine tubs
  • Plastic punnets/ ready meal trays
  • Glass bottles and jars
  • Jam jar lids (separate from the jars)
  • Food and drink cans

The following currently cannot be recycled:

  • Crisp packets
  • Plastic bags
  • Cling film
  • Polystyrene

Please place these items in with your household rubbish.

Replacement red and purple bags are available free of charge from one stop shops, libraries and leisure centres.
